WHAT IS TECHWIZ?

01. A tech-based competition conducted globally across all Aptech centers.
02. Tests software knowledge, proficiency, time and team management skills of students.
03. Gives students an opportunity to showcase their talent, win Cash prizes and more.
04. Evaluation by Aptech Head Office and Industry Experts



OBJECTIVE

. To help students experience the real-world environment, working in teams or individually within timelines.
.To give students an opportunity to work on a live project and create something new independently.
.To engage students with a global competition creating enthusiasm and interest.



INTRODUCTION

▪ Aptech has been one of the front-runners in the I.T. education space. In order to make learning interactive and exciting, Aptech initiated TECHWIZ in 2019.
▪ Techwiz is a Global IT competition which tests the technical prowess, proficiency, and time and team management skills of students globally.
▪ It is intended to help students experience the real-world environment, working in teams and developing Websites, Web Applications, Mobile Apps, and so on.
▪ There will be rewards for centers, faculties and mentors, and students. So, gear up for the ultimate tech face-off on a global level.

PROJECT CATEGORIES

The project categories include apps/Websites, which a team of 4 to 6 students will develop or create as per themes assigned by Aptech HO. The categories are as follows:

  • Website Design and Development
  • Web Application Development
  • Mobile App Development
  • IoT Solutions Development
  • Transforming Big Data with Data Science
  • Next-Gen Intelligent Solutions with AIML
  • Game Development
  • GenAI Smart Solutions

ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA


The eligibility criteria for participation in the project is given below:
Website Design and Development
• CPISM, CISE, Smart Professional students

Web Application Development
• DISM, HCSE & HDSE, Smart Professional students

Mobile App Development
• HDSE, HCSE, ACSE & ADSE, Smart Professional students

IoT Solutions Development
• ADSE, Smart Professional students

Transforming Big Data with Data Science
• ADSE, ACSE, ACN Pro, Smart Professional students

Next-Gen Intelligent Solutions with AIML
• ADSE, ACSE, ACN Pro, Smart Professional students

Game Development
• Game Development (Diploma/Higher Diploma/Advanced Diploma)

GenAI Smart Solutions
• ADSE (Data Science & AIML), ACN Pro (Data Science & AIML), Smart Professional (Data Science & AIML)



ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA


• Students can only participate in groups with a minimum of 4 and a maximum of 6 students.
• The Team or respective team member cannot participate in more than one category.
• If the Team or any student is found seeking local help/ staff assistance for the project, the team will be disqualified.
• The Team must submit the projects in the specified formats by the given deadline and any delay will disqualify the entry.
• If Team registered doesn't meet the eligibility criteria, it will be disqualified.
• Decisions from the jury appointed will be final for evaluation of projects & scores
